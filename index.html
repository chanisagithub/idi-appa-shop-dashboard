About ඉදි ආප්ප කඩේ

Located in the historic city of Galle, we are a passionate local food business dedicated to preserving and sharing the authentic flavors of Sri Lankan cuisine. Our specialty lies in crafting traditional string hoppers (idi appa) using time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

Every dish is prepared with the finest local ingredients and traditional cooking methods. Our string hoppers are made fresh daily, and our curries are slow-cooked to perfection, ensuring you experience the true taste of Sri Lankan home cooking. With a 4.6-star rating from over 100 satisfied customers, we take pride in delivering exceptional quality and authentic flavors.
